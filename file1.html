The Miss Southeastern Louisiana University pageant is a preliminary competition as part of the Miss Louisiana/Miss America Organization.

Southeastern's Campus Activities Board has sponsored the Miss Southeastern pageant since the 1970's. Each Miss Southeastern goes on to compete in the Miss Louisiana pageant. She gets to participate in many exciting events throughout the year as she represents Southeastern. If you would like Miss Southeastern at your event please contact the Campus Activities Board at CAB@southeastern.edu.

Performances brought to you by Southeastern's students and alumni.
